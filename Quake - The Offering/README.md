# Quake - The Offering - GOG + Epsilon
### Description:
Play "Quake - The Offering" provided by GOG on Linux!
### Technical notes:
- Epsilon enhanced packs v2.56
- 3 Mission Packs included: "Scourge of Armagon ", "Dissolution of Eternity" and "Abyss of Pandemonium"
